You need to understand your startup equity — dilution, vesting, SAFE notes, cap tables. But which tool should you use? Here's an honest comparison of the three most popular options.

Quick Overview

Carta

Enterprise cap table management platform. Built for companies that need 409A valuations, compliance reporting, and investor management. Used by 40,000+ companies. Pricing starts at ~$100/month for basic cap table management.

Pulley

Modern cap table management tool. Offers scenario modeling, fundraising tools, and cap table tracking. Simpler than Carta, focused on early-stage startups. Pricing starts at ~$100/month.

FounderMath (Free)

Interactive equity calculator suite for individual founders and employees. Visualize dilution, convert SAFE notes, compare job offers, calculate runway — all free, no signup required. Pro plan at $19/month for saving and exporting.

Best for Founders

For Founders

FounderMath wins for understanding "what happens to MY equity"

If you're a founder trying to understand how your 33% ownership changes after a seed round, a Series A, and an option pool creation — FounderMath is the fastest way to find out. Open the Dilution Calculator, enter your numbers, and see the results instantly. No signup, no setup, no cost.

Best for Startup Employees

For Employees

FounderMath is the only tool built for individual employees

Carta and Pulley are designed for companies, not employees. If you received a startup job offer and want to understand what the equity is worth, FounderMath has tools specifically for you:

  • Stock Option Calculator — What are your options worth at different exit scenarios?
  • Compare Offers — Side-by-side comparison of two startup job offers
  • Equity vs Salary — Is a lower salary + equity worth it?
  • Equity Quiz — Is your equity offer above or below market?
  • Vesting Calculator — When do you actually earn your shares?

None of these tools exist in Carta or Pulley — they're built for company administrators, not individual employees.

Best for Companies

For Companies

Carta or Pulley for formal cap table management

If you're a company that needs to issue stock options, manage 409A valuations, produce investor reports, and maintain an official cap table — Carta or Pulley is the right choice. These are full cap table management platforms with compliance features.

But many founders use both: FounderMath for quick scenario modeling ("what if we raise at $10M vs $20M?") and Carta/Pulley for formal cap table management. FounderMath is free, so there's no reason not to use it alongside your existing tools.
