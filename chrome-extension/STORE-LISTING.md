**Detailed Description:**

FounderMath is the free equity dilution calculator that startup founders actually need.

🧮 INSTANT DILUTION CALCULATIONS
Enter your starting ownership, number of funding rounds, and dilution per round — instantly see how your equity changes across each round.

📊 VISUAL DILUTION BAR
See your ownership shrink in real-time with a visual progress bar. Understand dilution at a glance.

✅ KEY FEATURES
• Quick popup calculator — no page navigation needed
• Supports 1-8 rounds of funding
• Adjustable dilution per round (1-50%)
• Visual dilution progress bar
• Links to 25+ free startup calculators

🔗 CONNECTED TO FOUNDERMATH
Get your full equity report with dilution charts, cap table analysis, and investor-ready formatting at founder-math.com. Free preview available.

Perfect for:
• Startup founders navigating fundraising
