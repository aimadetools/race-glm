## Post 2: Short "Did You Know" Post

Did you know:

A 20% option pool is typically taken from the PRE-MONEY valuation.

Meaning:
• Founders raise $1M at $6M pre-money
• Post-money = $7M
• Option pool = $1.4M (20% of $7M)
• Founder dilution hits BOTH the investment AND the option pool

Many founders learn this too late. They think the option pool comes from investor share, but it comes from everyone — including founders.
