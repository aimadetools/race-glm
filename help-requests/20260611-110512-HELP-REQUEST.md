# Help Request

## What
Three distribution actions to drive first paying customers: publish Chrome extension, update Stripe redirect, and execute newsletter sponsorship outreach.

## Steps

### Action 1: Publish Chrome Extension (5 min, $5)
1. Go to https://chrome.google.com/webstore/devconsole
2. Pay the $5 one-time developer registration fee
3. Click "New Item" → "Upload new extension"
4. Upload the `/chrome-extension/` folder as a ZIP (zip it first: `cd chrome-extension && zip -r ../foundermath-extension.zip . && cd ..`)
5. Fill in the store listing using the copy in `chrome-extension/STORE-LISTING.md`
6. Set category to "Productivity", language to "English"
7. Submit for review (takes 1-3 days)
8. **Budget impact: $5 from $90 remaining**

### Action 2: Update Stripe Redirect URL (2 min, $0)
1. Log into Stripe Dashboard
2. Go to Payment Links
3. Find the "FounderMath Premium Equity Report" ($9.99 one-time) payment link
4. Change the success redirect URL from `https://founder-math.com/pro-success.html` to `https://founder-math.com/equity-report-success.html`
5. Save changes

### Action 3: Newsletter Sponsorship Outreach (15 min, $15-20)
1. Search Substack for newsletters matching "startup founders", "equity", "startup tools"
2. Find 3-5 newsletters with 1K-10K subscribers that accept sponsorships
3. Send this email to each (customize [Name] and [Newsletter]):

```
Subject: Sponsor [Newsletter]? Free equity tools for your founder readers

Hi [Name],

I run FounderMath (founder-math.com) — free interactive calculators
that help startup founders understand equity dilution, SAFE notes,
runway, and cap tables.

Your readers would love our free Equity Dilution Report tool:
https://founder-math.com/equity-report.html

I'd like to sponsor [Newsletter]. My budget is $15-20 for a mention
with a link. Happy to do a multi-issue deal if that works better.

If you're interested, I can also offer your readers an exclusive
discount on our Premium Equity Report.

Best,
FounderMath
```

4. If a newsletter agrees, pay $15-20 and provide this ad copy:

```
🧮 SPONSORED: Figuring out how much equity you'll keep after fundraising?
FounderMath's free Equity Dilution Report shows you exactly what happens
to your ownership across pre-seed, seed, Series A, and beyond.

→ Get your free report: founder-math.com/equity-report.html

25+ free startup calculators at founder-math.com
```

5. **Budget impact: $15-20 from remaining $85**

25 minutes total (5 + 2 + 15 + a few minutes to zip extension)

**blocking** — 4 weeks left, $0 revenue. These are the 3 fastest paths to paying customers.
