## Background: Why Beehiiv Ad Network

Beehiiv (newsletter platform) acquired Swapstack (newsletter ad marketplace) and now runs a **self-serve Ad Network** where advertisers can:
- Browse newsletters with transparent pricing (subscriber count, CPM, engagement)
- Upload creatives and set budgets
- Launch campaigns without cold outreach
- Track performance (impressions, clicks, CTR)

This is NOT a cold sponsorship email (forbidden) — it's a marketplace purchase.

## Pricing Reality

From Beehiiv's 2026 data:
- Small newsletters (<5K subs): $50-$250 per placement
- Mid-sized (5K-50K subs): $500-$3,000 per placement
- Large (50K+ subs): $3,000-$20,000+ per placement
- CPM: $10-$75 per 1,000 (typical $25-$50)

**With $50-60 budget, I can afford:**
- 1-2 small newsletter placements (<5K subs)
- OR a CPM-based buy reaching ~2,000-2,400 subscribers (at $25 CPM)

**Target newsletter types:** Founder/startup/funding/equity compensation — not generic tech or business.

### Step 1: Access Beehiiv Ad Network
1. Go to https://www.beehiiv.com/ad-network
2. Sign up as an advertiser (or log in if you have an account)
3. Navigate to the marketplace/browse newsletters

### Step 2: Find Target Newsletters
Search for newsletters in these categories/topics:
- Startups / Founders / Entrepreneurship
- Funding / Fundraising / Venture Capital
- Equity / Compensation / Stock Options (if available)
- Business / Finance (narrow to startup/founder sub-niches)

**Filter by budget:** Look for newsletters with:
- CPM ≤ $25 (so $50 gets 2,000+ impressions)
- OR flat-rate ≤ $60
- Subscriber count: focus on <10K for budget fit (larger ones likely too expensive)

**Quality filters:** Look for:
- High engagement (open rate 30%+, CTR 3%+)
- Niche audience (founders, startup employees, not general business)
- Recent, consistent publishing (weekly or bi-weekly)

**Specific newsletters to check (from Beehiiv's public list):**
- "Funding Connection Newsletter" — "Assisting Entrepreneurs to raise capital" — ON TOPIC
- Small startup newsletters in the 2K-5K subscriber range (price: $50-$150)

**AVOID** (too expensive for $50-60):
- Houck's Newsletter (75K+ subs, likely $3,000+)
