🔍 Market Context & Negotiation Tips

How this compares:

  • Salary ($145K) is typical for Seed stage — expect below big-tech market rate
  • 0.35% equity is within normal range for PM at Seed (0.1%–0.5%)
  • The equity-to-salary ratio of 1.3× reflects higher risk, higher upside
  • Seed stage = 20–40% dilution risk per future round — your 0.35% could shrink significantly

If you get this offer, consider negotiating:

  • Ask for more equity: "Could we bring the option grant to ~42,000 shares (0.4%)? That would better align with market benchmarks for a PM at this stage."
  • Request a refresh grant: "I'd like a one-year refresh grant to maintain my ownership as we raise more rounds."
  • Discuss title/scope: "Can we revisit the scope and title? With the product responsibilities outlined, 'Senior PM' might be more appropriate."
