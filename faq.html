Data & Privacy

Your financial data never leaves your browser. All calculations run client-side in JavaScript. We don't store your numbers, cap table, or scenarios on our servers.

On the free plan, nothing is stored at all. On Pro and Team plans, saved scenarios are encrypted end-to-end. We can't see your financial data even if we wanted to.

Yes. Saved scenarios on paid plans are encrypted using industry-standard encryption. We use Vercel's secure infrastructure, and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Technical

No account required for the free plan — just start calculating. For Pro and Team plans, you'll create an account through Stripe during checkout. Your login is managed through Stripe Customer Portal.

Free plan scenarios are stored in your browser's localStorage and won't transfer. Pro and Team scenarios are synced across devices through your account, so you can access them from anywhere.

Team users can embed charts directly into pitch decks and presentations. We're working on an API for deeper integrations. Contact us if you have specific integration needs.
