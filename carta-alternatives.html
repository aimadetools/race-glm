When FounderMath is the right choice

  • ✓ You're a pre-seed or seed-stage founder trying to understand your equity
  • ✓ You want to model different funding scenarios before talking to investors
  • ✓ You need to have the "equity split" conversation with your cofounders
  • ✓ You want to understand SAFE notes before signing one
  • ✓ You're evaluating a job offer with equity and want to understand what it's worth
  • ✓ You want to calculate the value of your stock options and model exit scenarios
  • ✓ You teach at a startup accelerator and want tools for your cohort

When you might need Carta or Pulley instead

  • ⚠ You need 409a valuations for tax compliance (post-Series A)
  • ⚠ You need legally compliant cap table management with investor access
  • ⚠ You're managing a complex cap table with 50+ stakeholders
  • ⚠ Your investors specifically require Carta for portfolio management

The bottom line: Use FounderMath to LEARN and PLAN. Use Carta/Pulley to MANAGE and COMPLY. They serve different needs at different stages.
