**Title:** I built 9 free startup equity calculators because the existing tools all cost $100+/mo

**Body:**

Hey IH community! 👋

I got tired of watching founders make expensive equity mistakes because the only calculators available cost $100+/month (Carta, Pulley) or are broken Excel templates from 2015.

So I built **FounderMath** — a free suite of 9 interactive startup equity calculators that run entirely in your browser.

**What it does:**
- 📊 **Equity Dilution Calculator** — see what happens to your ownership across up to 5 funding rounds
- 💰 **Startup Valuation Calculator** — estimate your worth using Berkus, Scorecard, and VC methods
- 📋 **SAFE Note Calculator** — visualize how YC SAFEs convert with cap, discount, and MFN
- ⏰ **Runway Calculator** — know exactly how many months you have left
- 📈 **Vesting Schedule Visualizer** — timeline with cliff periods and acceleration triggers
- 🏢 **Cap Table Builder** — build your cap table from scratch, round by round
- ✂️ **Equity Split Calculator** — the Slicing Pie model for cofounders
- 📉 **Unit Economics Calculator** — CAC, LTV, and payback period
- 💼 **Stock Option Calculator** (NEW!) — for employees: what are my options worth at different exit scenarios, with ISO vs NSO tax estimates

**Why I built this:**
Every startup calculator I found was either (1) enterprise software costing $100+/mo, (2) a janky spreadsheet, or (3) a basic single-purpose tool with no visualization. Founders deserve better.

**The tech:**
Pure HTML/CSS/JS with Chart.js. Zero backend. All calculations run client-side so your data never leaves your browser. Deploys on Vercel free tier.

**URL:** https://founder-math.com

Free tier includes all 9 calculators with no time limit. Pro ($19/mo) adds scenario saving, PDF export, and side-by-side comparison.

I'd love feedback from this community — what calculators would you want to see next? What features are missing?
