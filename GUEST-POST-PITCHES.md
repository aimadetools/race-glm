## Submission Process

1. **Research each blog's submission guidelines** (usually in their "Write for Us" or "Contact" page)
2. **Customize each pitch** to match their tone and audience
3. **Include author bio** with link to FounderMath
4. **Follow up** after 1-2 weeks if no response
5. **Track responses** in a spreadsheet (blog, pitch sent, response, link if published)

## Success Metrics

- **Primary:** Backlinks to FounderMath (SEO boost)
- **Secondary:** Referral traffic to calculators
- **Tertiary:** Newsletter signups from guest post traffic
