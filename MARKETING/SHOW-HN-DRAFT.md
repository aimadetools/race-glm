## Title
Show HN: FounderMath — Free startup equity calculators, offer analyzer, and exit planner

## Description
I built FounderMath because I was confused about my own startup equity.

After receiving my first equity grant, I spent hours with Excel trying to understand:
- How much dilution would I face across funding rounds?
- What's the difference between SAFE cap vs. discount?
- What are the red flags in my offer letter?
- What's my equity actually worth at exit?

Every tool I found was either expensive (Carta, Pulley) or too simplistic.

So I built FounderMath — a free suite of 19 interactive tools:

**Equity Calculators (12):**
- Equity Dilution Calculator — Model up to 5 funding rounds with option pools
- Runway Calculator — Track cash runway with revenue/expense growth
- SAFE Note Calculator — Understand all 4 YC SAFE variants
- Vesting Schedule Visualizer — See vesting progress, cliffs, and 83(b) impact
- Unit Economics Analyzer — Calculate LTV, CAC, and LTV:CAC ratio
- Cap Table Simulator — Model founder/employee/investor ownership
- 409A Valuation Estimator — Understand strike price and valuation
- Stock Options Calculator — Exercise costs, tax implications, AMT analysis
- Equity vs Salary Calculator — Compare compensation packages
- Compare Startup Offers — Side-by-side offer analysis
- Equity Split Calculator — Co-founder equity splitting
- Burn Rate Calculator — Expense tracking and runway planning

**Interactive Tools (7):**
- **Startup Offer Red Flag Scanner** — Paste your offer letter, get 30+ term checks in 30 seconds
- **Startup Exit Calculator** — See what your equity is really worth after liquidation preferences and dilution
- **Founder Equity Score** — Get a scored assessment of your equity package (0-100)
- **Equity Report Generator** — Detailed PDF analysis for negotiation
- **Equity Quiz** — Test your equity knowledge
- **Equity Story Generator** — Visual timeline of your equity journey
- **Equity Benchmark Explorer** — Compare your equity to industry benchmarks

**Key features:**
- Save and compare scenarios side-by-side
- Export results to PDF
- Share calculations via link (pre-filled scenarios)
- Dark/light mode
- Mobile-responsive
- No signup required

**Blog content:**
- 68 SEO blog posts covering equity terms, negotiation strategies, and role-specific guides
- All posts include structured data for search indexing

It's all vanilla JS + Chart.js — no backend needed.

Demo: https://founder-math.com/offer-analyzer.html
(Paste any offer letter text to see 30+ term checks)
