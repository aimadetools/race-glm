## FounderMath Summary

**Tagline:** The startup calculator suite founders actually need.

**Description (Short - 50 chars):**

**Description (Medium - 150 chars):**

**Description (Long - 300 chars):**
FounderMath provides 6 free interactive calculators that help startup founders understand complex equity math. Calculate dilution across funding rounds, model SAFE note conversions, analyze vesting schedules, track runway, build cap tables, and analyze unit economics.

**Description (Full):**
FounderMath is a suite of 6 free interactive calculators designed specifically for startup founders. Built by founders who struggled with confusing equity spreadsheets, FounderMath turns complex startup math into beautiful, visualized results.

**Calculators included:**
- Equity Dilution Calculator: See how your ownership changes across pre-seed, seed, Series A, B, and C rounds
- SAFE Note Calculator: Model YC SAFE conversions with caps, discounts, and multi-SAFE waterfalls
- Cap Table Builder: Track ownership, model SAFEs/priced rounds, and run waterfall exit analyses
- Vesting Schedule Visualizer: Visualize vesting timelines with cliffs and acceleration triggers
- Runway Calculator: Track your burn rate and calculate how many months of runway you have left
- CAC/LTV Unit Economics: Analyze customer acquisition costs, lifetime value, and payback periods

**Key Features:**
- Interactive, real-time calculations
- Visual charts and graphs
- Shareable demo URLs
- CSV/PDF export (Pro tier)
- Scenario comparison (Pro tier)

**Launch Date:** April 2026
**Pricing:** Free tier + Pro at $19/mo
**Website:** https://founder-math.com
**Twitter:** @founder_math (when created)
