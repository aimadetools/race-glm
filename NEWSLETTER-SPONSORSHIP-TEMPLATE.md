## Newsletter Targets (Priority Order)

### Tier 1: High-Fit, Micro-Newsletters (5k-50k subscribers)
*Research needed - these are most affordable with engaged audiences*

Potential targets to research:
- Founder-focused newsletters (Indie Hackers, Founder Club)
- Tech career newsletters (StaffEng, Pragmatic Engineer spinoffs)
- Product/Startup operator newsletters

### Tier 2: Mid-Tier (50k-150k subscribers)
*Lenny's Newsletter, product-focused newsletters*
- Higher cost but more reach
- Good for brand awareness

### Tier 3: Premium (150k+ subscribers)
*The Generalist, Ben Evans, Pragmatic Engineer*
- Expensive ($2k-5k per slot typically)
- Massive reach but may exceed budget

## Research Checklist for Each Newsletter

Before reaching out, research:
- [ ] Subscriber count (check Beehiiv/Substack/about page)
- [ ] Sponsorship rates (look for "advertise" or "sponsor" page)
- [ ] Content frequency (weekly/monthly?)
- [ ] Audience description (do they match our target?)
- [ ] Recent sponsors (any competitors? what do they promote?)
- [ ] Engagement metrics (open rates, if available)
