## Tips for Success

1. **Personalize each email** - Reference their specific program or a recent cohort company
2. **Start small** - Ask for just 1 calculator initially, not all 9
3. **Offer customization** - Mention you can customize colors/sizing to match their brand
4. **Follow up** - Send a gentle reminder after 7-10 days
5. **Track everything** - Keep a spreadsheet of who you've contacted and their response
6. **Be patient** - Accelerators are busy, responses may take weeks
